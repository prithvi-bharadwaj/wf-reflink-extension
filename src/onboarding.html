Step 2 of 4 · The trick

Say it. Copy it. It lands.

1
You say: "Check out inserted link — so cool."
2
You press ⌘C on github.com/x/y
3
Wispr pastes: "Check out github.com/x/y — so cool."

Say "inserted link" or "embedded image" and press ⌘C while dictating. RefLink swaps each trigger with the next thing you copied, in order.
